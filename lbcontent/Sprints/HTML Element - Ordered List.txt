The difference is in the way the information is arranged.\n\nA paragraph simply displays text the way you type them, but a list arranges its content into items, each occupying a new line.\n\nThis is useful when you want to display information together to imply that they are related.\n\nIn this sprint we will look at the Ordered List, how it works and how to use it.\n\n=====\nClick Next to start
